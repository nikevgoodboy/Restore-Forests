The Importance of Tree Seed Conservation

Tree conservation begins with seeds, the foundation of preserving biodiversity and protecting forests. Seeds hold the future of our environment, enabling the growth of trees that provide oxygen, habitat, and stability to ecosystems. This page highlights the importance of conserving tree seeds, methods of seed collection, and how planting trees supports a healthier planet.
